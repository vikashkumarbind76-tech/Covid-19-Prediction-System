Health Protection Guidelines

Evidence-based recommendations for COVID-19 prevention

Precision Masking

Use a well-fitted N95 or KN95 mask in crowded indoor spaces. Replace if damp, damaged, or soiled for maximum protection.

Sanitization Routine

Disinfect high-touch surfaces regularly. Use 60%+ alcohol hand sanitizer and wash hands frequently for at least 20 seconds.

Improve ventilation, reduce close contact when symptomatic, and follow local public health guidance and isolation protocols.
